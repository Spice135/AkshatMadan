My Story

Over 6 years ago, I was sitting in my chair wondering what my future would look like. I was also wondering what the future of our world would look like. In that fleeting glimpse, I saw myself investing time, energy and effort along with applying and leveraging my creative and technical skills to create new technologies that would change our world and maybe even another world.

To me design is more than just a word or a theme. It has to be created and modified to function as an imperitive part of any project. Only a true designer could tell you that. To me code needs to be abstract with less dependencies to allow fast interpretation. With over 6 years of experience, I am starting to master the art of writing clean and concise code. The perfect inculcation of clean code, good design and creative ideas comes together to create a near-perfect work of art; be it a website, a company logo, or even a custom engine for a new video game.

It was in seventh grade, that I sat down in front of my Xbox and just thought: "What if I tried to create something like this?" From that thought, I have worked my way up and picked up programming and video game design. I then decided to pursue it as a career. I am now completing a Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science and Video Game Design at DigiPen Institute of Technology.
