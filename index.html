My Story

Who am I?

I am a software engineer and a game developer with a passion for accessibility. My goal is to create new and amazing experiences that affect my users lives in a positive way. Alongside that, I want to create applications that break barriers (physical, mental, linguistic, etc.) and make sure that my products are easily accessible by everyone, no matter what.
